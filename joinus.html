Orphans under your care may be sent at any moment to the RBDH hostel by their mothers or other relatives. Orphans who have been placed in the RBDH hostel will get free accommodation, religious guidance, care, schooling, food and drink during their stay.

The Guidelines for Adopting Orphans


The following are the policies for adopting orphans at RBDH:


1. Malaysia citizen

2. Children who have lost both parents.

3. Children that are ready for school range in age from 7 to 14.

4. Confirmation from a doctor that a child is healthy and not impaired.

5. A poor family with no willing near relatives to support them.

6. Children who are motivated to continue their education and better their lives and families in the future.



Applying for Admission


1. The heir obtains and completes the admission application form.

2. You may either download the form or pick it up at the RBDH office.
